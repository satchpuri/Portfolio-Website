Slime Spree is a local multiplayer base guarding game created in under 36 hours for the Microsoft Imagine Cup at RIT. Won 1st place for the best co-op category.
The goal of the game is to protect the treasure from enemy slimes by shooting slime bullets from your body which make you smaller or by using the special moves.

Role

  • Lead UI/UX Developer: Developed the UI, HUD and Sound Manager.
  • Gameplay Programmer: Worked with a team of 5 to design and develop special attacks for the main characters.

Tools Used

  • Unity 5.5/C#
  • Visual Studio 2015
  • Trello
  • Photoshop CC
  • Github
