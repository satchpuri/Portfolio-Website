BlackFeather a melee combat base guarding game where you play as a parrot who can control rival pirate gangs to protect your treasure and your island.
Currently in development by a team of 11 students, expected to be realeased in early May for the Imagine Cup at RIT'17

Role

  • Programming Head: Incharge of making sure the programming team is on track with their tasks.
    Conduct code reviews and ensure programming standards as well as help out in Gameplay or AI programming when needed.
    Using Agile conducting bi-weekly meetings to assess the progress of the team.
  • Lead UI/UX Developer: Developing the UI and the HUD for the game. Also working with shaders to give the a game Borderlands-style art.
